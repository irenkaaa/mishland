About

Welcome to Mishland, your number one source for all things - design your home. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of the comfort of your home, with an emphasis on fullfilling client's wishes with the best quality materials and attention to every detail.

Founded in 2018 by Irena Mishonova, Mishland has come a long way from its beginnings in Sofia. When Irena first started out, her passion for "Let you Feel your Home" drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
